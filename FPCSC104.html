Game Reviews


The Safe House rates games on the story and how immersive the story was, how fun the gameplay was and if the gameplay offered something new to the player Lastly, I view the game as if I'm completly new to the seris, that way, if you want to pick up the game, you know what it's gonna be like.

What the Safe house does


As a gamer for several years, I have often come across new game releases that are quite boring and when I look online, the game has a 10/10 review from other review sites. But when I look at what users are rating the game, like on youtube or steam reviews, they are also saying it's boring. So I wanted to make a site that was truely transpartant on how well the game/movie was and show where the flaws lie, whether in cinema or in the gaming industry.

Movie Reviews
