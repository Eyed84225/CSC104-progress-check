A review for Metal Gear Rising

Game Reviews Home Page Movie Reviews

Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance is one of, if not, the best Metal Gear game. From the unique gameplay mechanics, to the story, and one of the best soundtracks that have ever graced gaming. All of these element combine to create a one of the best Metal Gear games that I would give a 10/10 rating or 5 stars. I would whole heartly recommend this game for those who want to experience the metal gear franchise but don't like the stealth mechanics of the main series.
